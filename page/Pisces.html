Pisces

Febuary 19-March 20

            
MEANING

            

P  for psychic
I  for intelligent
S  for surprising
C  for creative
E  for emotionally-
           driven
S  for sensitive
    

    

    
Pisces is a watery sign , concerned with subtle emotions, and secret mystical depths.

They are friendly and likable, and yet can be very moodyand introspective as well.

Pisceans are not entirely at home in this world.

Pisceans are dreamy and full of imagination, emotionally sensitive, 
and easily influenced by their environment.
